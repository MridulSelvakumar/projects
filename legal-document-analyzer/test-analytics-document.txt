SAMPLE LEGAL CONTRACT

This is a sample legal contract for testing the analytics functionality of the Legal Document Analyzer.

PARTIES:
- Company ABC Inc. (the "Company")
- John Doe (the "Contractor")

PAYMENT TERMS:
The Company agrees to pay the Contractor $5,000 per month for services rendered.

TERMINATION CLAUSE:
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

CONFIDENTIALITY:
The Contractor agrees to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information.

LIABILITY:
The Company's liability shall not exceed the total amount paid under this agreement.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
All work product created shall be owned by the Company.

This contract is effective as of January 1, 2024.
